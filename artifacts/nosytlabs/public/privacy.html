Privacy

Privacy, in plain English.

Nosytlabs is a one-person studio (Nosyt LLC, US). nosytlabs.com is a single-page marketing site. Here is exactly what gets collected when you visit, and why.

What we collect

Google Analytics 4 (GA4): anonymized page views, country-level location, browser and device class, referrer. IP addresses are anonymized at collection (we set anonymize_ip: true). No cross-site tracking, no advertising profiles, no remarketing.

The subscribe and contact forms are routed through formsubmit.co: the email address you type, plus your message and topic for the contact form. Submissions arrive in the hi@nosytlabs.com inbox. We never sell, share, or feed them to a third-party CRM.

Server logs from the hosting platform: standard request logs (timestamp, status code, user agent) for security and debugging. No personal profile is built from these.

What we don’t collect

No cookies for advertising. No fingerprinting. No session replay. No third-party social pixels. Embeds on the page (Spotify) load directly from the provider and are governed by their privacy policies, not this one.

Your choices

You can block the GA4 script with any standard ad/privacy blocker — the rest of the site works fine. To opt out of email replies after subscribing, reply to any note with “unsubscribe” and we’ll remove your address within 48 hours.

Contact

Privacy questions? Email hi@nosytlabs.com — a real person reads it.
