How working together goes

  1. You email a one-paragraph problem. No deck, no intake form. hi@nosytlabs.com — a real person reads it.
  2. You hear back within two business days with either a fit/no-fit, an honest scope question, or a draft estimate.
  3. If we’re a fit, you get a fixed-scope written quote: deliverables, timeline, terms, and what would change the price.
  4. We build in the open by default. Private repos when the work is yours, public ones when it’s a generic tool that the rest of the world should also have.
  5. You own the code. Repository transferred to your org on completion, with a written README so the next engineer (or your future self) doesn’t need us to keep it running.

What we won’t take on

Honesty is part of the offer. We turn down: ad-tech surveillance work, AI “ghostwriting” passed off as human, NDA-only secret projects with no portfolio rights at all, and anything that needs to ship in under a week. The studio works on focus — “urgent” for someone else can’t be urgent for everyone.
